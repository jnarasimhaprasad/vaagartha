वागर्थ

Vāgartha  ·  word and meaning

वागर्थाविव संपृक्तौ वागर्थप्रतिपत्तये । जगतः पितरौ वन्दे पार्वतीपरमेश्वरौ ॥

Kālidāsa · Raghuvaṁśa 1.1

Streams

Mahābhārata — A Retelling महाभारतम्

Evening by evening, Appaa tells the epic to Niveditā and Nīlakṛṣṇa — not as a lesson, but as something first seen, then felt, then understood. Śravaṇa becoming darśana.
